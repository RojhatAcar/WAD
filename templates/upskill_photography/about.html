About Upskill Photography

Upskill Photography is a website constructed for the Computing Science Web App Development team project in the University of Glasgow.

Upskill Photography was designed to be a platform for beginner, amateur, and professional photographers who want to have a community to share their photography work, get feedback from other photographers on their pictures, or get inspired by the works of others.
